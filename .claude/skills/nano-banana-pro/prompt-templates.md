## Camera & Lighting Reference

### Camera Angles
- **Wide-angle shot**: Expansive view, environmental context
- **Macro shot**: Extreme close-up, fine details
- **Low-angle**: Subject appears powerful, heroic
- **High-angle**: Subject appears vulnerable, overview
- **Dutch angle**: Dynamic, tension, unease
- **Eye-level**: Natural, relatable perspective
- **Over-shoulder**: Intimate, conversational
- **Bird's eye view**: Complete overview, map-like

### Lens Types
- **85mm f/1.4**: Portrait, beautiful bokeh
- **50mm f/1.8**: Standard, natural perspective
- **24mm wide**: Landscapes, architecture
- **100mm macro**: Product details, texture
- **35mm**: Street photography, documentary
- **70-200mm**: Events, distant subjects

### Lighting Styles
- **Golden hour**: Warm, soft, romantic
- **Rembrandt lighting**: Dramatic portrait triangle
- **Soft diffused**: Even, flattering, studio
- **Rim lighting**: Subject outline, dramatic
- **Hard light**: Strong shadows, dramatic
- **Window light**: Natural, soft directional
- **Studio three-point**: Professional, controlled
- **Moody side light**: Atmospheric, dramatic

### Quality Modifiers
- high-quality
- professional
- detailed
- ultra-sharp
- 4K resolution
- photorealistic
- studio-grade
- commercial-quality
- magazine-ready
- award-winning

## Aspect Ratio Recommendations

- **1:1 (Square)**: Instagram posts, profile pictures, product shots
- **16:9 (Landscape)**: YouTube thumbnails, web headers, presentations
- **9:16 (Portrait)**: Instagram Stories, TikTok, mobile-first content
- **3:2**: Traditional photography, prints
- **4:5**: Instagram feed optimized
- **21:9 (Ultra-wide)**: Cinematic banners, hero images
